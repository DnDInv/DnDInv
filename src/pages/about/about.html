Welcome to the About page of the
D&D Inventory app.
You can swipe left and right to move
between the information pages.

Small Tutorial: 

To get started with this app,
you can go to the homepage and start
adding your first Backpack by
pressing the button with the "+" on it
To successfully create a backpack the
following things are required:

1. Backpack name
2. Strength of the backpack (1 to 20)
3. The Size of your character
4. The rule of your backpack

Those are the Required fields to create
the backpack without any failure.
